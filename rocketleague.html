Rocket League

  • Genre: Vehicular soccer
  • Release Date: July 7, 2015
  • Developer: Psyonix

Gamemodes

Soccar - Standard gamemode
Snow Day - The ball is a puck
Hoops - Goals are hoops
Rumble - Players get a random powerup every 10 seconds
Dropshot - Put the ball into the floor instead of standard goals

Playlists

Casual - Duel (1v1), Doubles (2v2), Standard (3v3), Chaos (4v4)
Competitive - Solo Duel (1v1), Doubles (2v2), Standard (3v3)
Extra Modes - Snow Day (3v3), Hoops (2v2), Dropshot (3v3), Rumble (3v3)
